About Us



Our Story

Established in 1998, Eat&Run was born out of a passion for food and a commitment to excellence. Our founder, "Abhishek Bhardwaj", envisioned a place where people could come together to savor delicious flavors, celebrate special moments, and create lasting memories. What started as a humble eatery has now blossomed into a beloved dining destination, cherished by locals and visitors alike.







Our Philosophy

At Eat&Run, we believe in the power of fresh, high-quality ingredients, sourced locally whenever possible. Our culinary team, led by Executive Chef "Sanji", is dedicated to crafting dishes that showcase the best of seasonal produce, sustainable seafood, and artisanal meats. Each recipe is thoughtfully created, combining traditional techniques with modern twists to deliver flavors that tantalize the taste buds..
